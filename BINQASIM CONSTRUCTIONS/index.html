About BinQasim Constructions

Local, family-led construction firm focused on high-quality residential builds across Karachi. We combine practical design, reliable schedules and durable construction so each home stands the test of time.

Our approach

We work directly with homeowners to understand needs and budget, then produce a clear plan: site survey, layout options and a transparent cost estimate. On site we ensure quality through daily supervision and milestone checks.

Materials and workmanship

  • Concrete and masonry built to local best practice
  • Waterproofing and damp proofing suitable for Karachi climate
  • Quality finishes: tiles, paints, sanitary ware and carpentry

Services

Complete construction services — from initial concept to final handover.

Design & Planning

Site survey, layout optimization, building permit guidance and realistic cost planning.

Concrete & Structural Works

Foundations, columns, beams and structural framing executed with strong quality control.

Finishes & Fit-Out

Tiles, paints, carpentry, electrical and plumbing—finished to client expectations.

Renovation & Extensions

Project Management

Procurement, timeline control and daily supervision to keep projects on schedule.

Aftercare & Warranty
